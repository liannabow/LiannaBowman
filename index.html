About Me

I have a Bachelor's degree from the University of Michigan in the Program in the Environment, and a CIT Developer certificate from Northwestern Michigan College. In addition to front-end development, I have an understanding of object-oriented programming principles, relational databases, and networking technologies.

I was thrilled to discover that web development is the perfect combination of my logical and artistic abilities. My goal is to combine my passion for a healthy environment with my development skills. I believe my diverse background, motivation, and strong attention to detail will be an asset anywhere I go.
